My Favorite Travel Destination in Kerala

Munnar

Munnar is a locality in the Western Ghats in Kerala in southwest India . It is known for the tea plantations that surround the place for miles and is therefore a popular tourist destination. The place is a hill station that was a popular summer resort for military and civil servants alike during the British colonial era. Munnar lies at the confluence of Muthirappuzha , Nallathanni and Kundaly rivers at an altitude of approximately 1500 to 1600 meters. The name Munnar was therefore probably derived from the Malayalam words munu (“three”) and aru (“river”). The Munnar panchayat has an area of ​​nearly 557 km², making it the largest panchayat in Idukki district . It belongs to Devikulam block .
